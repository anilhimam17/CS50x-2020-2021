This is a startup entirely focussed on innovation and creation of new technology. Technology that can help the world and the all the people in it.

We further focus on helping an making the lives of people modern and futuristic through our products but at the same time use the technology ourselves. Thus making the technology a natural extension of an individual.

Lastly our core fundamental is to make technology which is easily recyclable as we care about our planet. We want to make technology which dosent harm the planet and at the same time is incrediblely powerful.



Key Areas of Focus

  1. Artificial Intelligence

    We think that AI is the key to the future of this planet and all its living beings.
    With AI we could cure diseases like the novel coronavirus.
    With AI we could try and resolve the mother of all challenges our race has faced yet -- Climate Change. We believe that if want our race to be interstellar, then first we need to take good care of our planet so that we continually have the resources to make our race inter-planetary.

  2. Internet of Technologies and Industrial IOT

    We believe that IOT is a technology which will play a key role in the future.
    In the near future our lives are to become more agile and the only technology which can make other technologies agile is IOT (For Homes) and AIOT (For Offices and Industries).

  3. Electronic Devices

    We think that life in the future is possible only and only if we have a carefully created colletion of electronic devices. Devices whose technology is similar but not the same and whose features meets different needs and not the same.

    Only if we have a clear thought on the way this collection works then we can create technology that is powerful and reduces e-waste.
